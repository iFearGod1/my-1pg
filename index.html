Stress Management: 60-Second Reset

A quick reset you can do anywhere. No equipment. Just a calm way to lower intensity and regain focus.

Start:

  • 1) Breathe (20s)
    Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds.
    Exhale slowly for 6 seconds. Repeat.
  • 2) Release (20s)
    Drop your shoulders. Unclench your jaw.
    Relax your hands. Let your breath stay slow.
  • 3) Re-focus (20s)
    Pick ONE task. Say: “Next, I do ____.”
    Then start for just 2 minutes.

Why does slower exhale help?

  • Longer exhales can activate the parasympathetic (“calm”) response and reduce stress intensity.

What if I’m anxious and can’t slow down?

  • Start smaller: inhale 3 seconds, exhale 4 seconds.
  • The goal is steadier breathing, not perfection.

Best habits to reduce stress long-term?

  • Sleep consistency, daily movement, hydration, sunlight, and limiting constant notifications.
  • Small wins beat big resets.

A quick “reset list” I can follow daily?

  • Water + 5 minutes walking + 1 deep breathing set + single-task for 10 minutes.

Nutrition: Fuel for Training & Recovery

Nutrition plays a foundational role in energy, recovery, and long-term performance. Below are simple, evidence-based choices that support training without overcomplicating it.

Discover More in Our FAQs

Protein supports muscle repair, recovery, and satiety. Aim for a consistent amount across meals.
Carbs are performance fuel. Favor whole-food sources and adjust based on training volume.
Healthy fats support hormones and long-term health. Prioritize olive oil, nuts, seeds, avocado, and fatty fish.
Hydration impacts energy and focus. Build a simple habit: water with every meal + before training.

Time Management: The 90-Minute Focus Block

Instead of managing an entire day, focus on managing one high-quality block of time. This system reduces overwhelm and increases follow-through.

THE 90-MINUTE BLOCK

10 minutes — Setup
Clarify one task. Close distractions. Decide what “done” looks like.
60 minutes — Deep Focus
Work on one thing only. No switching. No notifications.
20 minutes — Shutdown
Save progress. Write the next step. Step away.
