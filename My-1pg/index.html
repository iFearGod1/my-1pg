Stress Management: 60-Second Reset

A quick reset you can do anywhere. No equipment. Just a calm way to lower intensity and regain focus.

Start:

  • 1) Breathe (20s)
    Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds. Exhale slowly for 6 seconds. Repeat.
  • 2) Release (20s)
    Drop your shoulders. Unclench your jaw. Relax your hands. Let your breath stay slow.
  • 3) Re-focus (20s)
    Pick ONE task. Say: “Next, I do ____.” Then start for just 2 minutes.

Time Management:
The 90-Minute Focus Block

Instead of managing an entire day, focus on managing one high-quality block of time.

THE 90-MINUTE BLOCK
• 10 minutes — Setup
Clarify one task. Close distractions. Decide what “done” looks like.
• 60 minutes — Deep Focus
Work on one thing only. No switching. No notifications.
• 20 minutes — Shutdown
Save progress. Write the next step. Step away.
